To play the game, simply enter a number in the box below. When you submit it you'll see the sequence of all whole numbers from 0 to your number, with some zany substitutions mixed in. Can you figure out the pattern the substitutions follow?

Any number containing a 1 is replaced by "Beep!"

Any number containing a 2 is replaced by "Boop!"

Any number containing a 3 is replaced by "Won't you be my neighbor?"

Numbers with a 3 take precedence over numbers with a 2, which take precedence over numbers with a 1.
